Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the loan approval take?

For personal loans, approval typically takes 24-48 hours. For secured loans like home loans, it may take 5-7 working days depending on document verification.

Is there any fee for using GetLoanOffer?

No, our services are completely free for borrowers. We are paid by the lending partners, so you don't have to pay us anything.

What credit score is required for a loan?

Generally, a credit score of 650+ is considered good for personal loans. For home loans, lenders prefer a score of 700+. However, we can help you explore options even with lower scores.

Can I apply for multiple loans?

Yes, you can apply for multiple loans if your income and credit profile support it. However, we recommend discussing with our experts to understand the best approach.

How is my data protected?

We take data security very seriously. All your information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with unauthorized third parties.
